# LoudWake — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-14_

LoudWake is designed to respect your privacy completely.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** LoudWake does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal
data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party
SDKs that track you.

## Where your data lives

Everything you create in LoudWake — your alarms, their times, labels, sounds,
and challenge settings — is stored **only on your device** (in the app's local
storage and a private App Group container). It never leaves your phone and is
never sent to us or anyone else.

## Permissions

- **Alarms (AlarmKit):** used solely to schedule and ring your alarms.
- **Motion:** used solely to count shakes/steps for the wake-up challenge. Motion
  data is processed on-device in real time and is never stored or transmitted.

## Children

LoudWake does not collect data from anyone, including children.
