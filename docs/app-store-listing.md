## Description
LoudWake is built for people who sleep through normal alarms.

Most alarms are too easy to dismiss — a tap, a button, and you're back asleep.
LoudWake won't let you. It rings loud, vibrates relentlessly, and refuses to stop
until you actually wake up and prove it.

WAKE-UP CHALLENGES
Choose what you have to do before the alarm turns off:
• Math — solve arithmetic problems (adjustable difficulty)
• Type a phrase — type a sentence exactly
• Memory — repeat a sequence from memory
• Shake — shake the phone until you're moving

NO EASY ESCAPE
• No snooze button
• Continuous vibration that the volume buttons can't silence
• The alarm isn't dismissed until every challenge is solved

BUILT RIGHT
• Reliable scheduling with Apple's AlarmKit
• Multiple alarms with repeat days and labels
• Pick your alarm sound and difficulty
• Preview an alarm before you trust it with your morning
• A clean, calm, modern design

Set it tonight. Actually get up tomorrow.

## App Review notes (paste into the "Notes" field — important)
LoudWake is an alarm clock for heavy sleepers.

- Alarms are scheduled with AlarmKit.
- The audio background mode is used to play the alarm sound at the scheduled time
  and to keep the alarm sounding reliably while the app is backgrounded, which is
  the app's core function — like other alarm apps.
- To dismiss an alarm the user must complete a wake-up challenge (math/typing/
  memory/shake), which is the app's whole purpose: preventing oversleeping.
- No account or login is required. The app collects no data.

To test: open the app, tap "Preview alarm" to experience the challenge flow
immediately, or create an alarm 1–2 minutes out and lock the device.
